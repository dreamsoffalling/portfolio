About Me

Hi! I'm Isabella "Cas" Hancock, a current senior at Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD). I'm studying Game Development and Game UX, and am expected to graduate June 2025.


Finding ways to effectively communicate information is my passion! Going through the process of user research and group feedback to create engaging and creative interfaces is where I find the most enjoyment. I hope to take my learned skills and transform them into something great. My biggest dream is to be a part of a project that impacts others in a memorable way.


I've interned seasonally for 4 years at Section 127 - an Indianapolis-based graphics design company that specializes in the sports industry. It has taught me the timeline and flow of the creative field, and has strengthened my overall work ethic and output. I'm excited to take what I've learned and apply it to my future endeavors.
